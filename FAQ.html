Frequently Asked Questions

  • What payment methods do you accept?
    We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex), PayPal, and Apple Pay.
  • How long does shipping take?
    Orders are processed within 24 hours. Standard shipping takes 3–7 business days; expedited options are available at checkout.
  • Can I return or exchange a product?
    Yes, you may return any product within 30 days as long as it is in unused condition. Please visit our returns page for detailed instructions.
  • How do I track my order?
    Once your order ships, we’ll email you a tracking number. You can track your order on our website using your account dashboard.
  • Where is your customer support located?
    Our support team is based in Mumbai, India and is available Monday to Saturday, 9 am – 7 pm IST. Reach us by phone, email, or live chat.
